About the Project

Based on 2-3 years of intensive R&D at the Golestan Ischemic Disorder Lab, the SALAR ecosystem provides a first-principles approach to cardiac electrophysiology. All software is developed in Delphi, focusing on Pure Time-Domain Processing to ensure zero signal-history dependency and high-performance real-time analysis.
